What to do at the Biolumic Shores of Vaadhoo Island in the Maldives:

The Bioluminescent Shores of Vaadhoo Island in the Maldives are famous for their glowing blue waters at night. Visitors can walk along the beach and see the waves light up with natural bioluminescence, swim in the sparkling sea, and enjoy the magical, otherworldly atmosphere.
