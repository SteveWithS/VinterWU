What to do at the Pyramids of Giza:

The Pyramids of Giza in Egypt are ancient wonders and iconic symbols of the Pharaohs. Visitors can explore the massive pyramids, see the Great Sphinx, learn about Egypt’s fascinating history and architecture, and even go inside some of the pyramids.

It’s an unforgettable experience that connects you to one of the world’s oldest civilizations.
