What to do at the Colosseum:

At the Colosseum in Rome, you can explore the ancient amphitheater where gladiators once fought, walk through the arena floor and underground passages on guided tours, learn about Roman history in the exhibition areas, and enjoy impressive views of the structure from different levels.

Many visits also include access to the nearby Roman Forum and Palatine Hill, giving you a fuller picture of ancient Roman life.
